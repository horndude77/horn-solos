Errata

Below are errors which have been found in the printed book. They have been fixed in the digital version above.

Cherubini Sonata No.1

  • Measure 9, beat 1 should be a 'B'
  • Measure 10, beat 2 should be a 'D' (tied to a 'D' on beat 3)

Mozart Horn Concerto No.2

  • Movement III, Measure 85 should have 'E' 'D'.

Mozart Horn Concerto No.4

  • Movement III, Measure 169 last note in measure should be a 'C'.
